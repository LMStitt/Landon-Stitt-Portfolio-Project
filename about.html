About Me

Background, interests, and goals in geography and GIS.

About Me

I am a geography student with a focus on geographic information systems and spatial analysis. My coursework includes cartography, remote sensing, and applied GIS, and I have experience with both desktop and web-based mapping platforms. I am interested in using spatial data and visualization to support planning, research, and decision-making.

Academic background

I am pursuing a degree in geography with an emphasis on GIS and spatial analysis. My studies include thematic cartography, spatial statistics, and geospatial technology. I have completed projects in site suitability, data collection and editing, and map design using industry-standard software.

Career interests

My career interests center on GIS, urban geography, and spatial analysis. I am drawn to roles that combine mapping, data analysis, and clear communication of spatial patterns. I aim to work in planning, environmental analysis, or related fields where geographic information supports better outcomes.

Goals

I aim to build a strong foundation in GIS and spatial analysis, develop clear and accessible cartographic and analytical deliverables, and contribute to projects that use geography to inform decisions. I am committed to continued learning and to applying best practices in data quality, documentation, and visualization.
